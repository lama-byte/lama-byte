## About Me

I'm Lama Alowaydhi, a Senior Cybersecurity student, driven by a genuine curiosity about the technology that powers and protects our digital world.

What first drew me to cybersecurity was its essential role in the technologies we use every day. As our world becomes more connected, I believe everyone should understand how to stay safe and confident in the digital environment. This interest naturally led me to explore different areas of the field, particularly network security, cryptography, and secure software design. I enjoy building systems that are not only secure but also user-friendly, because I believe security should empower users rather than create obstacles for them. At the same time, I find great satisfaction in analyzing vulnerabilities, understanding how attacks work, and discovering effective ways to defend against them. Whether I'm designing a solution, investigating a weakness, or exploring a new technology, I enjoy approaching problems with curiosity and a continuous desire to learn.

More than anything, I enjoy exploring different areas within cybersecurity. I'm constantly experimenting, building, learning, and challenging myself. While I continue to discover where my long-term specialization lies, I'm enjoying every step of the journey and embracing the opportunity to grow into a well-rounded cybersecurity professional.

***Outside of academics, I value creativity, problem-solving, and lifelong learning—qualities that continue to shape both my personal and professional development.***
